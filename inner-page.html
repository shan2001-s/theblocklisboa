About us


Back in 2017, a group of crypto friends shared the urge to create a workspace for like-minded individuals.

Not long after, The Block was born. Since the very beginning we’re constantly searching for improvement. We’re always progressing along the challenges that come up. This is how we create our dynamic, open and vibrant coworking space.

Nowadays we can claim to be one of the first sites in Lisbon that focuses entirely on the crypto community. However, we will most welcome non-professional crypto fans and enthusiasts from different areas as well. The doors of The Block are open for all people who want a comfortable working space in the center of the city.

At The Block you will be able to enjoy a diversity of working spaces. From proper desks with ergonomic chairs, to standing tables and stools. And do you want to stretch your legs and relax? Make yourself comfortable on the couches in some of the areas.

Our rooftop terrace has an amazing micro-climate. This is where we welcome internal and external events. If you are looking for a venue to host an event, please feel free to reach out. We'll get back to you shortly with a proposal.

The kitchen is fully equipped with all you need for making a strong cup of coffee or even to cook a meal. Just store your stuff in the fridge and make yourself at home!

Feel like you want to detach a bit from your computer? Join our health and wellness program. Members use it either to exercise during a yoga class or to relax during a professional massage.

Our Crypto Fridays are famous! During this weekly event we invite the community to come together, share ideas and meet like-minded individuals. Do you want to check out the vibe next Friday? Join our community on Meetup and follow all the upcoming events.

Our location


We are located in Picoas, a central commercial area in Lisbon. We are close to two metro stations, and you can find anything around our offices - from great restaurants and cafés to post offices and supermarkets. Everything you need just around the corner!
